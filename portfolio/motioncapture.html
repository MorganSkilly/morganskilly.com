Motion Capture Projects

Throughout my time at Abertay I have carried out a number of motion capture shoots for my own projects as well as that of external university partners. These projects have involved various motion capture technologies including Vicon, OptiTrack, and markerless capture systems.

My motion capture work spans across multiple disciplines including performance capture for character animation, biomechanical analysis, and real-time virtual production applications. Each project has provided unique challenges and learning opportunities in the field of motion capture technology.

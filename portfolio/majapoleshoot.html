Pole Dancing Motion Capture and Virtual Production Shoot

This MSc project was led by Maja Oszczypała. As the on-set virtual production technical artist running the volume I ensured everything ran smoothly on the day of the shoot and solved problems as they arose. As well as running the virtual production volume I also assisted with various expermiental motion capture sessions, testing different marker placements and configurations to achieve a good quality capture of the performance.

Unreal Engine Helicopter VFX Sequence

By blending a mixture of traditional VFX methods and real-time game engines, I created a short VFX sequence with live action drone footage. This project demonstrates the integration of drone cinematography with Unreal Engine's real-time rendering capabilities to create dynamic aerial sequences.

The workflow involved capturing drone footage, camera tracking, and compositing with CG elements rendered in real-time within Unreal Engine. This hybrid approach allowed for flexible iteration and creative control throughout the post-production process.
